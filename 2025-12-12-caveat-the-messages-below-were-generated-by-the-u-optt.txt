⏺ Summary

  I've analyzed the SDC staleness checking code and written a plan to PLAN.md. Here's the overview:

  The Problem

  compute_all_stale_cells() runs O(n) expensive Checkpoint.diff() operations after every cell execution. In a 50-cell notebook, that's 50 diffs per execution.
